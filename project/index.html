About Us

Born during the Civil Rights Movement at Morgan State College, Iota Phi Theta Fraternity, Inc. has grown into a powerful force, impacting communities while keeping its five principles at heart. With chapters across the nation and around the world, we continue making a difference everywhere—always “Building a Tradition, Not Resting Upon One!”
